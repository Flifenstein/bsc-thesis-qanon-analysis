# Into the Q storm: sentiment and thematic analysis of QAnon online communities

My BSc thesis (2021, BSc Communication Science, University of Twente).

A mixed-methods study of three online communities where QAnon discourse circulated: QAnon-related boards on 8kun, r/QAnon_Casualties, and r/Qult_Headquarters. The work combined Python-based scraping, R-based sentiment analysis, and qualitative thematic coding.
